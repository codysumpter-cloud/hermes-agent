# Content Growth Social Ops

## Overview

This skill helps Hermes and Buddy turn approved source material into platform-native content plans for YouTube, X, and Twitch. It can analyze positioning, generate hooks, draft posts, prepare YouTube metadata, build Twitch promo beats, and create approval checklists.

It must not post, reply, DM, delete, change profile settings, access credentials, or spend money directly. All external actions remain adapter-mediated and approval-gated.

## When to Use

Use this skill when the user wants viral content strategy, content repurposing, upload checklists, promotional calendars, short-form concepts, X threads, Twitch stream announcements, or cross-platform content loops.

Do not use it for direct account login, automated engagement, spam, impersonation, mass messaging, or platform manipulation.

## Workflow

1. Confirm the approved source material, audience, platform, and goal.
2. Extract the core promise, proof, novelty, and emotional hook.
3. Generate platform-native drafts: YouTube title/description/tags, X posts or threads, Twitch stream title/category/promo copy.
4. Mark each output as draft, scheduled candidate, or requires approval.
5. Produce a final review checklist before any external action.

## Common Pitfalls

- Do not optimize for virality by encouraging deception, harassment, or spam.
- Do not reuse copyrighted material without clear rights.
- Do not claim a post was published unless an approved adapter confirms it.
- Do not bypass Prismtek approval for external actions.

## Verification Checklist

- Drafts are platform-specific.
- Claims are supported by user-provided or cited source material.
- External actions are clearly separated from planning.
- Approval requirements are explicit.
- No secrets, credentials, cookies, or auth tokens are requested.
