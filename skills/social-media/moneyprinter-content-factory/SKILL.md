# MoneyPrinter Content Factory

## Overview

This skill converts MoneyPrinter-style modular content automation into a safe Buddy/Hermes content factory. It can plan short-form videos, draft X posts, structure affiliate content, prepare outreach drafts, and create approval checklists.

It must not auto-post, auto-message, scrape private data, impersonate people, hide sponsorships, or perform account actions.

## When to Use

Use this skill when the user wants repeatable content-production workflows, content idea queues, short-form scripts, campaign plans, or draft-first promotion systems.

## Workflow

1. Identify source material, offer, audience, platform, and approval boundary.
2. Generate hooks, scripts, captions, thumbnails notes, and repurposed drafts.
3. Mark any affiliate or sponsorship claim for disclosure review.
4. Create a manual approval checklist before publishing or outreach.

## Common Pitfalls

- Do not create spammy mass outreach.
- Do not imply automation has posted anything.
- Do not reuse third-party content without permission.
- Do not bypass platform rules or Prismtek approval.

## Verification Checklist

- All outputs are drafts.
- Disclosure needs are flagged.
- No credentials or cookies are requested.
- External actions are blocked unless approved through Buddy adapters.
